Welcome to My Portfolio

Welcome to my digital portfolio! Here, you’ll find a collection of my work and experiences in the field of accessibility and web development. I believe that digital spaces should be inclusive and accessible to all users, and this portfolio is a reflection of that philosophy. Whether you’re exploring my projects, reading my insights, or checking out my resume, I hope you see my dedication to creating user-friendly, accessible digital experiences. Feel free to explore my work, and reach out if you’d like to connect or collaborate!

About Me

I’m Carl Neubert, an Accessibility Subject Matter Expert and Web Developer with a passion for creating inclusive digital experiences. Over the years, I’ve worked with diverse teams to integrate accessibility best practices into the development process, ensuring that web applications are accessible to everyone — regardless of ability.

My journey into accessibility began with a personal interest in creating a more inclusive digital world, and it has since evolved into a professional mission. I have hands-on experience with WCAG 2.2, Section 508, ARIA, and various assistive technologies like screen readers, making me well-versed in the needs and challenges faced by users with disabilities. When I’m not working on improving web accessibility, you can find me writing, cycling, and advocating for better digital practices.

I’m always looking to connect with others who are passionate about making the web more accessible. Feel free to explore my portfolio, and let’s talk about how we can make the web a more inclusive place for all users.
